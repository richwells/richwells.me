Christmas Sketches

27 Dec 2013

Every year on the Sunday before Christmas, my wife’s parents invite all the elderly single people at their church to join them for a Christmas dinner. There’s lots of sherry, laughter, stories being shared and helpings of bread and butter pudding being enjoyed (a desert they request every year!). It’s great to join in with the fun and capture some of the incredible character of people who’ve been around for a little while longer than me!

Making art that no one sees

25 Oct 2013

We recently got back from a two week holiday in Cornwall, and before we left, my wife and I decided to take a break from social media and emails. We removed from our phones all icons which spit out numbers in red dots and made a pact not to touch them until we got back. It’s something we’ve done on holidays before and have always found it refreshing to properly disconnect from what’s going on at home and get some perspective. Recommend it.
