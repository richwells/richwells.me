Healthy Island is a fun, replayable, game for children ages 7â€“11 years old. It encourages them to think about health and wellbeing choices in three areas: exercise, healthy eating and dental health.

This app has been developed as part of the Schools App Challenge 2016 by West Wakefield Health & Wellbeing Ltd, a network of six neighbouring GP practices in Wakefield, West Yorkshire.

Children in schools across the are were involved in the project. They created initial concepts and design ideas with the help of some peer-to-peer judging and feedback from mentors and local GPs.

Healthy Island currently features three quizzes and one game. We will be adding two more games later this year.
