Give to Tearfund, get some artwork

03 Apr 2014

I’m posting a limited edition signed print (1 of 60) to anyone who gives £3/month to our Tearfund Cambodia challenge.

The inspirationI saw these two children cycling past our mini bus in one of the villages we visited. I loved their expressions and the way they worked together to keep the tiny colourful bike upright. It was a great picture of how Tearfund are supporting communities to face uncertain futures together. I had fun creating this illustration and have tried to capture some of the vibrancy and texture that I saw all around me.
